As Seen on Media

Lore Olympus is a romance webcomic created by New Zealand artist Rachel Smythe. The comic is a modern retelling of the relationship between the Greek goddess and god Persephone and Hades. It began publishing weekly on the platform Webtoon in March 2018. Lore Olympus is currently the most popular comic on Webtoon; as of March 2024, it has 1.4 billion views and 6.5 million subscribers. The comic has won two Eisner Awards. two Harvey Awards. and two Ringo Awards. It was announced in 2019 that a television adaptation was under development..

Major Characters in Lore Olympus

Hades

The God of the underworld, often depicted as a handsome blue man in a business suit, managing the Underworld Corporation.

Persephone

The goddess of spring, depicted with pink skin, who faces challenges that change her from an innocent to a strong woman.

Zeus

The king of gods, a yellow-skinned man whose actions often bring chaos to Olympus due to his power and corruption.

Poseidon

The God of the sea, Zeus' brother, known for his carefree and loyal personality, with green skin and wavy hair.

Eros

The God of love, depicted as a maroon-skinned young man, and a close friend of Persephone.
